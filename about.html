Introduction
I'm Vishal Shelake, a twenty year old Freelance iOS Developer from a small city of India called Nashik.
My interest first provoked when I saw my cousin reading programming books as she was pursuing her engineering exams. She briefly explained me what it all was and since then I fell in love with it
My Journey
It all began soon after I realised my interest in computers.
I requested my cousin to help me enrol in classes where I can learn programming so she made me join 'C' language classes. I was just 14 when the process of learning initiated & the more I’d learn the more curious I was getting and followed by 'C' I learned Python.
Contemporaneously events kept happening that fascinated me more towards knowing more about software related things. One day my father bought an iPhone 4 and me and my cousins went crazy about the apps and feel of it. Observing all the craziness we were having made me realise that future is going to be dominated by these phones and gadgets.
Back to learning stage 2, I opted for computer science as my major for class 11th and 12th. While everyone in 12th were decided which college to get in to, I was still looking for add on’s to my knowledge. After some research I came across this bootcamp (DevMountain) in US that teaches you iOS Development and being a fanboy of Apple and technology this was a dream come true to learn about it in a proper way.
In early 2018 I graduated from DevMountain (View my certificate), and since then I’ve been doing projects as freelancer and working for my startup (Read below).
My Startup - Stackinterop (Full-Stack iOS Development)
After coming back from my bootcamp in United States, I realised that my city is way behind others in terms of technology, most of the sectors here are still using tools and softwares that were made 2-3 decades ago & some are still using pen, papers for their daily operations !
Waking up with this fact everyday, I made myself a promise that I would use all my knowledge I've acquired throughout to bring as many changes and updates I can for my society.
From past 7-8 months, me and some of my friends are working on a startup called Nuclei, which is a set of 3 Apps working synchronously to bring solutions for retail and wholesale businesses.
Nuclei is my first attempt to bring a technological change in my society and currently we are trying hard to make everyone aware about this. Success or failure of Nuclei would never be a measurement for taking more steps forward.

The Leaderboards
The story behind my first group app
The major source of all my ideas being supplied from is one of my personality trait which is being observant.
So during my time at DevMountain, all the people around including me, had this crazy love for ping pong & FIFA which was installed in lobby and everyone used to play it during lunch breaks and after-classes. But as always everyone usually fought over whoâ€™s the best at a particular game?
To which the solution came up in my mind, why not build an app which tracks who has played how many matches, won, loss etc.. and so the entire group can see that In order to make competition more interesting and fun.
So in order to graduate everyone had to make a group project, and I presented this app idea and along with 2 other members of my cohort we made this a reality and we won the award for best group App, and what an honour it was to be leading a group with my own idea and winning.
The winner app got the price of having its app icon in DevMountain Salt Lake City's campus hall of fame iPhone Wall which was started just before my cohort.
If you and your friends are also fighting over who's really the best... You can solve the conflicts by downloading The Leaderboards from the App Store for free.
